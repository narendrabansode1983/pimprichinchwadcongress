Our Ideology

The five pillars that guide our path towards an inclusive and progressive Pimpri Chinchwad.

Democracy

Upholding the democratic values and institutions of our great nation.

Secularism

Respecting all religions and fostering communal harmony across PCMC.

Inclusive Growth

Ensuring development reaches the last person — no citizen left behind.

Social Justice

Fighting for the rights of the marginalized and working class of PCMC.

Nationalism

Unwavering commitment to the unity, integrity, and progress of India.
