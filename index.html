About Us

Congress in Pimpri Chinchwad

Dedicated to the people of PCMC — from Pimpri to Chinchwad, Akurdi to Nigdi.

🏛️

The Pimpri Chinchwad City Congress Committee has been working tirelessly for the development of one of Maharashtra's fastest-growing industrial cities. Our party represents workers, farmers, women, youth, and every section of society.

🏭

Industrial Workers' Champion

Supporting the rights and welfare of lakhs of workers in the PCMC industrial belt.

🎓

Education & Youth

Fighting for quality education, scholarships, and employment opportunities for local youth.

🌿

Environment & Infrastructure

Clean roads, green spaces, and smart infrastructure for a liveable PCMC.

👩

Women Empowerment

Our Agenda

Our Priorities for PCMC

Congress commits to these key development goals for Pimpri Chinchwad.

🚰

Clean Water for All

24x7 clean drinking water supply to every home in PCMC at affordable rates.

🛣️

Better Roads

Pothole-free, well-lit roads across all 68 prabhags with proper drainage systems.

💼

Local Employment

Policies to ensure PCMC industries hire local youth and pay fair wages.

🏗️

Affordable Housing

Low-cost housing schemes for workers and middle-class families in PCMC.

🌳

Green PCMC

Urban forests, parks, and anti-pollution drives to improve air quality.

📶

Digital City

Free WiFi zones, e-governance, and smart city solutions for every ward.
