ABOUT US
🏛️

INC Pimpri Chinchwad

The Indian National Congress, Pimpri Chinchwad city unit, is dedicated to serving the people of PCMC with unwavering commitment to justice, equality and development.

With a legacy rooted in India's freedom struggle, we continue to fight for the rights of workers, farmers, youth and every citizen of our industrial city.

Our mission is to build a progressive Pimpri Chinchwad where every resident has access to quality education, healthcare, employment and dignified living.

OUR IDEOLOGY

Democracy

Upholding democratic values and institutions of our great nation.

Secularism

Respecting all religions and fostering communal harmony in PCMC.

Inclusive Growth

Ensuring development reaches the last person — no citizen left behind.

Social Justice

Fighting for the rights of the marginalized and working class of PCMC.

Nationalism

Unwavering commitment to the unity, integrity and progress of India.
